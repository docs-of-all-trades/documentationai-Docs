## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- A valid email address for account verification
- Optional: An existing account on GitHub for integration (advanced feature)

<Callout kind="info">
  No credit card or payment information is required to start. You can create unlimited documentation spaces on the free tier.
</Callout>

## Sign Up for Konstantinos K

Follow these steps to create your account quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com`.

    Click the **Sign Up** button in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email address, full name, and a secure password.

    Click **Create Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Konstantinos K.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

A documentation space organizes your project docs. Set one up now.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Spaces" icon="folder">
    Log in to `https://dashboard.example.com`.

    Click **New Space** from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`.

    Choose visibility: Public, Private, or Team.

    Select **Create Space**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add and Edit Your First Document

Populate your space with content using the built-in editor.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Visual Editor" icon="edit-3">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create Document">
        In your space, click **New Document**.

        Name it `Introduction.mdx`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Edit Content">
        Use the WYSIWYG editor to add headings, lists, and images.

        Preview changes in real-time.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Markdown Editor" icon="code">
    Switch to Markdown mode for advanced formatting.

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
````mdx
---
title: Introduction
description: Welcome to my docs
---

## Hello World

This is your first document!
````
````markdown
# Hello World

This is your first document!
````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Konstantinos K supports full MDX syntax including components like `<Callout>` and `<Steps>`. Escape special characters like `{variable}` with backticks in prose.
</Callout>
