## Quick Steps

1. Sign in to your Konstantinos K account.
2. Click "New Project" in the dashboard.
3. Enter your project name (e.g., "My Docs").
4. Click "Create".

## Manage Your Space

- Add pages using the menu.
- Organize sections for your docs.

You're now ready to add and edit content in your documentation space.
