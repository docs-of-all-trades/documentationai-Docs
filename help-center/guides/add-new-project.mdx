description: Guide to adding a new project in Konstantinos K.
---

## Overview

Organize documentation by creating separate projects.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to dashboard">
    Click on your profile at the top right and select "Dashboard".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create project">
    Click the "New Project" button.
    Enter a name and optional description.
    Click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start documenting">
    Select your new project to begin adding pages and sections.
  </Step>
</Steps>
